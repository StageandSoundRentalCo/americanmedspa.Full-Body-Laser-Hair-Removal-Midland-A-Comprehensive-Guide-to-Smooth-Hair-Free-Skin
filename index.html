How to Prepare for Your Full Body Laser Hair Removal Treatment

To ensure the best possible results, proper preparation is essential. Here are some tips to follow before your appointment:

  • Avoid Sun Exposure: Stay out of direct sunlight and tanning beds for at least two weeks before your treatment.
  • Shave the Area: You may be asked to shave the area you want to be treated before your session. Avoid waxing or plucking, as the laser needs to target the hair follicle.
  • Avoid Certain Skin Products: Refrain from using lotions, creams, and other skin products on the day of your treatment to avoid irritation.
  • Consult with Your Specialist: During your consultation, discuss any medications or skin conditions that might affect your treatment.
